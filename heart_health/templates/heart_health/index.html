Learn About Your Cardiovascular Health!


You will discover:

  • Your risk score for heart disease based on a random forest machine learning algorithm trained and tested on a dataset of 70,000 people and statistical 2-proportion z testing for risk factors

  • Cardiovascular screening frequency recommendations based on your personal risk for severe heart disease and a cost benefit economic model

  • Your geographic risk based on your state's deaths from cardiovascular disease as compared to other states

  • Educational articles from PubMed, WebMD, and Google Scholar personally curated to your risk for cardiovascular disease, related risk factors, location, and literacy level (using natural language processing)
